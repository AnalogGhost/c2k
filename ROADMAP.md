# Roadmap

Future improvements — not yet prioritised.

## Audio & Feedback

- Customisable countdown warning intervals (currently fixed at 10s and 5s)
- Beep/chime option as an alternative to voice announcements
- Announce current pace or distance mid-interval

## Training

- Custom workout builder (user-defined intervals)
- Rest day reminders / workout scheduling
- Heart rate zone guidance (manual input, no wearable required)

## Progress & Stats

- Streak display on history screen (currently only on home)
- Weekly/monthly summary view
- Personal bests per program week
- Total distance and time milestones

## Maps & GPS

- Route map view in history (GPX is exported but not visualised)
- Live map during workout

## UX

- Widget for quick workout start from home screen
- Wear OS companion for wrist prompts
- Treadmill mode (disables GPS, shows effort cues instead of pace)
- Dark/light theme toggle (currently follows system)

## Accessibility

- Larger text / high contrast mode
- Haptic pattern variety (different vibration patterns per interval type, not just on change)

## Internationalisation

- More translations (French, German, Portuguese are obvious gaps given the user base)
